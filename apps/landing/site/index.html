How the temple works

Three acts. No small print.

Streaming taught the world to rent everything forever. We went back to how cinema always worked — you buy a seat, the lights go down once, and the person who made the film gets paid.

Act I

Buy your ticket in Naira

Pick a film or a series and pay once — card or transfer, priced by the filmmaker. A series ticket unlocks every episode.

Act II

Take your seat

Watch on iPhone or Android in a secure, watermarked player. One watch per ticket — every episode plays exactly once, like a real premiere.

Act III

The maker gets paid

Up to 90% of every ticket settles to the filmmaker. No middle men, no fine print — the box office, rebuilt for Nollywood.
